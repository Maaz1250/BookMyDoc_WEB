About BookMyDoc

BookMyDoc is a smart, mobile-first clinic management and appointment booking app designed to simplify healthcare access in both rural and urban areas. Built by a team of passionate student developers from Uka Tarsadia University, our mission is to empower clinics and patients with digital tools that reduce waiting time, ensure transparency, and improve medical service quality.

Our app offers features like adaptive appointment rescheduling, role-based access for staff and doctors, analytics, and upcoming AI-powered first-aid suggestions — all developed using Flutter and Firebase technologies.

Privacy Policy

At BookMyDoc, we are committed to protecting your personal health information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

All your information — including appointment records, prescriptions, and medical history — is encrypted and securely stored using industry-standard tools like Firebase Firestore. Our platform uses secure login mechanisms and role-based access to ensure that only authorized users (Doctors, Admins, Staff) can view specific data.

Any AI-based suggestions provided by BookMyDoc are based on anonymized or permission-granted data only. You may request to review, modify, or delete your data at any time via the app's settings or by contacting our team.

For questions or concerns regarding data usage, please contact us through the app or email us at shaikhmaaz1250@gmail.com.
